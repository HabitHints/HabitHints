Overview / About

Welcome to HabitHints, the ultimate tool for building better habits and transforming your life! Inspired by James Clear’s bestselling book Atomic Habits, HabitHints takes the principles of effective habit formation and combines them with cutting-edge technology to deliver a truly personalized experience.

Our app goes beyond simple habit tracking. HabitHints acts as your personal coach, helping you identify triggers, focus on small, achievable actions, and align your habits with the person you want to become. With real-time feedback powered by AI, insights tailored to your lifestyle, and a vibrant community for motivation, HabitHints is here to make lasting change not just possible, but effortless.

Start small, stay consistent, and let HabitHints guide you every step of the way. It's time to unlock your full potential—one habit at a time.

Features



  • Personalized Habit Recommendations
    Tailored suggestions to fit your lifestyle and goals, making it easy to start the habits that matter most to you.
  • Micro-Habit Tracking
    Focus on small, actionable steps that are simple to achieve, helping you build momentum effortlessly.
  • AI-Powered Feedback
    Receive real-time guidance and encouragement to stay on track and refine your habits as you grow.
  • Progress Visualization
    See your growth with clear, interactive charts that track your journey and celebrate your achievements.
  • Daily Reminders & Notifications
    Get timely nudges to keep you consistent without being overwhelming or intrusive.
  • Community Support
    Join a supportive network of like-minded individuals who can motivate and inspire you along the way.
